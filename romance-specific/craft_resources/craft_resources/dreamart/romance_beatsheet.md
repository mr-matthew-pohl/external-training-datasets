## NOTES AND VARIATIONS

### SUBGENRE-SPECIFIC CONSIDERATIONS
- **Contemporary**: Focus on realistic modern issues and contexts
- **Historical**: Period-appropriate challenges and courtship norms
- **Paranormal/Fantasy**: Integrate supernatural/magical elements with relationship development
- **Romantic Suspense**: Balance danger/mystery with developing relationship
- **Erotic Romance**: Integrate character growth with sexual journey

### STRUCTURE VARIATIONS
- **Cinderella Structure**:
  - Act 1: Meet hero (unhappy/cynical) and heroine (exceptional but in desperate situation)
  - Act 2: Circumstances force them together; hero is skeptical but eventually realizes his love
  - Act 3: Hero saves heroine with his love

- **Beauty and Beast Structure**:
  - Act 1: Meet stable heroine and rough-edged hero who falls for her
  - Act 2: Circumstances force them together; she hesitates but begins falling for him
  - Act 3: Heroine saves hero with her love

- **Dual Timeline Romance**:
  - Present day romance interwoven with past timeline
  - Emotional resonance between timelines
  - Resolution that brings timelines together

- **Reverse Structure**: Black moment occurs early, story focuses on rebuild/healing/forgiveness
